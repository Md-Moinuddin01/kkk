About

Built for people who need another way to speak.

SignSpeak AI is designed for people who cannot speak, have speech difficulties from birth, or communicate more comfortably through hand signs. It keeps the interface calm, direct, and forgiving so the technology feels supportive instead of demanding.

The camera is used only in your browser to find hand landmarks. The detected sign can be shown as large text, mapped to a personal response, and spoken aloud through the Web Speech API.
